Types of Food

There are many kinds of fast food. What will one place have in terms of fast food types, depends on what grows there, what of ingredients can be obtained and what local people like. Here are some types of fast food:

  • Hotdog
  • French Fries
  • Hamburger
  • Chips
  • Pizza
  • Taco
  • Chicken Nuggets
  • Onion Rings
  • Cheeseburger
